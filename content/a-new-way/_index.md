The response to the ad-driven web is already happening. It is not fringe, not crypto-utopian, not dependent on any single technology. In lots of different places, people are building the same kind of thing: direct relationships between people who make things and people who value them.

If you have arrived here from the front page, you already sense that something about the current model is off. This section is not here to convince you. It is here to show you that the thing you were quietly thinking is actually a shared practice with real momentum.

The sections below are being written. When complete, they will cover:

- Decentralised social infrastructure and the ATProto ecosystem
- Cooperative and reader-funded journalism
- Open source sustainability initiatives
- Community-funded creator models
- Collective and institutional funding

Each section profiles a pattern, not a platform. The goal is to show what is working, what is hard, and what you can do to participate. Where relevant, specific communities and projects are named — including [at.fund](https://at.fund), which is one tool among several in the ATProto ecosystem, built by the same team that maintains this site.
